# AMMOR Demo Script

## Demo flow

1. Start with compliance and security posture summary.
2. Show claims ingestion and case creation.
3. Demonstrate evidence upload and AI score generation.
4. Show AI Analyze -> Human Review auto-transition.
5. Show decision explanation and audit evidence links.
6. Show dashboard health and risk alerts.

## Gate statements

- All evidence shown should use non-sensitive test data.
- Confirm no credentials or live identifiers are visible.
- Confirm rollback and incident handling are documented.

## Demo acceptance

- Reviewer can explain why each transition is governed.
- Reviewer can trace a sample case from intake to final decision.
- Reviewer can find risk score and audit evidence quickly.
