# AMMOR Government Portal Guide

## Priority requirements

- Strict auditability.
- Clear role-based access.
- Deterministic case state transitions.

## Operational recommendations

- Keep AI explanation text immutable once submitted for review.
- Keep evidence links and chain IDs visible to auditors.
- Verify human review gate is mandatory before final decision changes.

## Common checks

- Login and permission checks for public-sector roles.
- Evidence upload integrity and hash capture.
- Decision log completeness.
